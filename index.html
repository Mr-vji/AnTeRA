For the first time in Hyderabad - Andhra, Telangana & Rayalaseema delicacies under one roof !!

AnTeRa Kitchen & Bar on Road No-10 Jubilee Hills aims to bring you the culinary & traditional legacies of Andhra, Telangana & Rayalaseema to your palate with amazing drinks.

We aim to serve the original flavourful delicacies of 3 regions with authentic spices & ingredients straight from the Telugu heartland. For those who are in the mood to relish delicious local Telugu cuisine with an uptown traditional ambience, this will be their favourite go-to restaurant.

While you feast on our flavourful delicacies, you can club it with delicious drinks of your choice.

Tala Hua Gosht

This dish looks like a delicious Tala Hua Gosht, a spicy and flavorful lamb curry from the Indian subcontinent. The meat is cooked in a rich, aromatic gravy with a blend of Indian spices. It is often served with naan or roti.

Pomfret Fish Fry

This dish looks like a delicious Pomfret Fish Fry! It's a popular coastal Indian dish where the fish is marinated in a blend of spices, coated in a flavorful batter, and then fried until crispy and golden brown. The fish is often served with lemon wedges and green chilies.
